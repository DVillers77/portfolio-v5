About Me

Bridging the gap between tactile empathy as a Massage Therapist and high-performance digital systems.

The Auditor's Eye: Bridging Empathy and Engineering

After 20 years of auditing human systems as a Massage Therapist, I pivoted to UX Engineering after an AI-driven career audit identified a personality-to-career alignment of 98%. This confirmed a lifelong pattern: from building a self-designed Graphic Communications degree to auditing educational materials for effectiveness, my career has always been about understanding how complex systems serve people.

Systems Thinking & Technical Mastery

I specialize in building robust, human-centered systems using BEM methodology, DRY principles, and WCAG AA accessibility standards. My education at The Tech Academy focused on problem-solving logic from the bottom up. I believe that technical mastery is the foundation of empathy—good code ensures that digital solutions are inclusive, performant, and reliable.

Why UX Engineering?

Massage therapy taught me that you cannot fix a symptom without understanding the underlying system. UX Engineering is the digital equivalent of that philosophy. I don't just "fix" interfaces; I build scalable architectures. By leveraging AI to accelerate development and streamline developer handoffs, I ensure that my designs are not just feasible, but high-performing.
